Borsch

Description

Make a warming bowl of borsch (also known as borscht or borshch) with this simple and comforting recipe. Serve this hearty soup with a dollop of soured cream and a chunk of crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 1kg fatty beef shin, (left in large chunks)
  • 1 onion, peeled and left whole
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. To make the stock, put the meat, whole onion, bay leaf and 2 litres of lightly salted cold water in a large saucepan. Break up any larger pieces of beef into the broth, remove the whole onion and discard.
